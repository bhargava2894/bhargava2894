## 🚀 Projects

### 📒 [Bill Buddy](https://github.com/bhargava2894/Bill-Buddy) — AI Bill & Credit Management for Kirana Stores

> Scan a bill. AI reads it. You confirm. Done.

![Bill Buddy Dashboard](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bhargava2894/Bill-Buddy/main/screenshots/dashboard.png)

Full-stack web app for Indian kirana stores to manage supplier bills, track vendor payments, record customer credit (udhaar), and export GST reports — all powered by Google Gemini AI.

- 📸 Upload a bill photo → Gemini Vision extracts vendor, amount & due date automatically
- 📒 Khata — per-customer udhaar ledger with running balance & one-tap settlement
- 📊 GST input reports + PDF & Excel export
- 🌐 Multi-language: English · हिन्दी · తెలుగు

**Stack:** `React 19` `Spring Boot 3` `PostgreSQL` `Google Gemini AI` `Framer Motion` `Java 17`
