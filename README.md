### [📒 Bill Buddy](https://github.com/bhargava2894/Bill-Buddy)

> AI-powered bill management for Indian kirana stores

- 📸 **AI Bill Extraction** — Google Gemini Vision reads vendor, amount & due date from a photo
- 💸 **Vendor Payments** — Track overdue bills, pending payments, payment history
- 📒 **Khata** — Per-customer udhaar (credit) ledger with one-tap settlement
- 📊 **GST Reports** — Generate reports + export to PDF / Excel
- 🌐 **Multi-language** — English · हिन्दी · తెలుగు

**Stack:** React · Spring Boot · PostgreSQL · Gemini AI · Framer Motion
